Professional journey

Mohammed Sazzad Kamal Chowdhury is a seasoned agricultural entrepreneur and founder of Miniforest Agro Farms, with over a decade of experience in tropical agriculture. His professional journey began in the land of Fatikchari, where he cultivated his first plantations and developed a deep understanding of soil management, sustainable farming, and local crop ecosystems. Prior to this, he gained substantial business experience as a successful entrepreneur in Dubai and earned his MBA in India, equipping him with international business acumen and strategic management skills.

Under his leadership, Miniforest Agro Farms integrates traditional agricultural wisdom with modern cultivation science, expanding operations into tea estates, rubber plantations, high-value nursery stock, and diversified crop production. His approach emphasizes discipline, ecological stewardship, and sustainable growth, ensuring that each hectare under management remains productive while preserving long-term soil health.

Mr. Chowdhury advocates for minimal-till practices, integrated pest management, and precision agriculture, applying science-backed methodologies to optimize yields, resource efficiency, and environmental responsibility. He actively contributes to the agricultural sector through service on advisory boards, promoting knowledge-sharing, innovation adoption, and community empowerment. His leadership ensures measurable outcomes in production, quality, and community impact, reflecting a commitment to long-term excellence and responsible land stewardship.
